Location Rules

How Alerts Change by Place

The alert type changes automatically based on the selected camera location type.

School

Only weapon alert and crowd alert are enabled for focused school safety.

College

All alerts are enabled: weapon, crowd, fire, smoke, traffic, safety and suspicious activity alerts.

Mall

Public safety alerts such as crowd, weapon, emergency and restricted item alerts.

Railway Station

Useful for crowd monitoring, suspicious activity and emergency evidence records.

Road

Supports road-related events such as helmet, triple riding, seat belt and high-speed alerts.
